About Me

Outside of programming, I like to play sports, watch TV and documentaries, and hang out with friends and family.

UBMe, Inc

Principle Engineer

UBMe's mission is to create Hyperlocal Communities allowing people to communicate and socialize. UBMe has an App on the App Store and Play Store that allows people to do this. UBMe also has a business platform that allows businesses to promote themselves by creating a Hyperlocal Community themselves. Since I have been with the company, I have worked on expanding this functionality to Event Organizers as well. In addition, I have created a Curbside Communication for businesses to continue serving their patrons safely.

Optum Inc, Employer eServices Portal

Full Stack Engineer

Employer eServices is a solution for company administrators to manage their employee healthcare plans. Since joining the team, I've worked on several different projects, including the ability for users to request temporary medical id cards as PDFs, the content migration from Vignette to Adobe Experience Manager, and integrating Optum ID, Optum's umbrella identity management system, into many different parts of the portal.

Optum Inc, Cirrus Software Engineer

Backend Software Engineer

Cirrus is Optum's large-scale healthcare solution that automates claim adjudication, benefit coverage, and member administration. I developed RESTful APIs used for claims processing, searching members' claims, and member profile managment under platform's Member microservice domain.

Optum Inc, OptumCare Delivery Portal

Test Automation Engineer

OptumCare Portal is a portal where patients communicate with physicians for primary care, specialty care, and post-acute care. I generated automated test scripts to team's API services used to search for patient and physician information including history, PCPs, and demographics.
